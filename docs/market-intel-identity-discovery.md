# TCOS Market Intel™ Licensed-Card Identity Discovery

This slice closes the empty exact-identity gap without loosening Market Intel accuracy rules.

## Flow

Tracked licensed-pro player → broad eBay discovery → professional/non-base filters → parsed candidate → admin review → exact identity → normalized listing → score/alerts.

## Safety

- No base cards.
- No college, NIL, amateur, pre-pro, custom, reprint, logo-less, or unlicensed products.
- Discovery candidates cannot receive deal alerts.
- Approval requires year, licensed manufacturer/product, exact card number, and a non-base signal.
- Approval creates or reuses the exact identity and then ingests the live listing through the normal scoring gateway.
